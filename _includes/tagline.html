Automating Data Collection and Validation for Federal Agencies

We are creating shared components for The Institute of Museums and Library Services.

As part of this effort, we are building and demoing a prototype in the open to explore automation for the Public Library Survey. We aim to generalize this shared components work for other Federal agencies in the future.

Through the judicious use of APIs and automation, we believe we can give 800 years of yearly effort back to librarians, and therefore their local communities.
